    X Education generates a large number of leads through various online channels, 
    but only about 30% of these leads convert into paying customers, resulting in inefficient use of sales resources. 
    The company currently treats all leads equally, leading to time and effort being spent on low-potential prospects.
     The key problem is to identify and prioritize high-quality leads that are more likely to convert. Therefore, there 
     is a need to build a data-driven solution that can assign a lead score to each prospect based on their likelihood of conversion, 
    enabling the sales team to focus on the most promising leads and improve the overall conversion rate toward the target of 80%.
